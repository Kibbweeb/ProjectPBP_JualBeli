Who We Are

MadFragrance is a socially and environmentally progressive fragrance craft brand dedicated to creating unique scents that empower individuals to express their true selves. Our mission is to cultivate strength and confidence through the art of fragrance, allowing our customers to embark on a sensory journey that resonates with their inner spirit.

Inspired by the mythical world of Murim, our fragrances are designed to evoke the essence of heavenly demons, blending boldness with elegance. At MadFragrance, we believe that a great scent is more than just a fragrance; it's an experience that activates your senses and enhances your presence in the world.




Where we are

MadFragrance HQ is located in the city of Hajun, Shaanxi, China. Where the essence of heavenly demons is captured in every bottle. Come visit us to experience the magic of our fragrances.

Our Values

At MadFragrance, our core values are deeply rooted in social responsibility, environmental sustainability, and the celebration of individuality. We are committed to crafting fragrances that not only captivate the senses but also contribute positively to the world around us.

We prioritize ethical sourcing of ingredients, ensuring that our products are cruelty-free and environmentally friendly. Our dedication to sustainability extends to our packaging, which is designed to minimize waste and reduce our carbon footprint.

MadFragrance is more than just a brand; it's a movement towards embracing one's true self through the power of scent. We believe in the transformative ability of fragrance to inspire confidence, evoke emotions, and create lasting memories. Join us on this journey as we cultivate strength and activate your scent.
